Code Plateau

8 months full-time immersive Bootcamp

Designed for young people who want to start their career in IT and kickstart their portfolio of real-life projects that utilizes modern technology stacks.

Why Code Plateau?

Human Capital Development

This is one of the key focus of the rescue administration, the Plateau State Government through the Plateau State ICT Development Agency has initiated the Code Plateau Initiative as a platform for re-skilling our youth who are interested in kickstarting their careers in IT and preparing them for the Job marketing by providing them a collaborative learning environment with all-around support from technical to industry job readiness.

Job Placement

At the end of the Code Plateau Fellowship, fellows are expected to be able to build impressive, portfolio-ready projects that will demonstrate their ability to add value to their new job as software engineers. This intensive program is for those who are committed to having tangible, job-ready skills upon graduation.

1st Half (3 Months)

The first half (3-months) of the fellowship based on this curriculum is described as “Floating Surface” because of how much information it packs.

2nd Half (3 Months)

Called “Diving Deep”, you use your new skills to build projects, while learning new technologies on the fly. At the end of the fellowship, you will be an autonomous engineer, capable of tackling unique, unfamiliar problems and building complex applications.

Last 2 Months

There would be internship placements of fellows across tech companies within Nigeria, this is to give the required on-the-job experience in tech companies to finally prepare our fellows for Job placements.

Plateau State ICT Development Agency has therefore prepared a world-class curriculum to prepare our youths for the global competitive IT job market.
